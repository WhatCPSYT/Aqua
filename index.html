Bot Features

Advanced Moderation

  • Commands: warn, mute, unmute, kick, ban, unban
  • Voice Moderation: vcmute, vcunmute, vcdeafen, vcundeafen, vckick
  • Full warning system with history tracking and /warnings command
  • Automatic unmute/undeafen after timer expires
  • Safe and reliable timeout-based moderation

Global AutoMod & Security

  • Keyword filters, anti-spam, and mention limits
  • Global link blocking and automatic message filtering
  • Fully automated AutoMod rule creation with /automod commands
  • Professional logging system (who used command, target, reason, timestamps)

Music & Entertainment

  • Advanced music player – supports search, links, and easy control
  • Controls: play, pause, skip, and more
  • Auto-disconnect after inactivity with text-channel notification

Data Management & Privacy

Your Data, Your Rights

Aqua stores moderation-related data (warnings, ticket setups, logs). You have the right to:

  • Request Data: Get a copy of all stored info regarding your user or server.
  • Delete Data: Request permanent removal of all your data from our system.

To exercise these rights, join our Support Server and open a Data Request Ticket.

DM Policy: Aqua will never send unsolicited DMs. DMs are sent ONLY for Ticket Transcripts. No ads, no spam.
