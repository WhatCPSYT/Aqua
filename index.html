Data Management & Privacy

Your Data, Your Rights

Aqua stores moderation-related data (warnings, ticket setups, logs). You have the right to:

  • Request Data: Get a copy of all stored info regarding your user or server.
  • Delete Data: Request permanent removal of all your data from our system.

To exercise these rights, join our Support Server and open a Data Request Ticket.

Voice & Chat Privacy: Aqua does not record or listen to your voice conversations. Similarly, no messages are ever recorded or stored; content is processed in-memory only for AutoMod filtering and immediately discarded.

DM Policy: Aqua will never send unsolicited DMs. DMs are sent ONLY for Ticket Transcripts at your request.
