Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

Aqua Bot collects minimal data necessary for functionality:

  • Server IDs (to store settings)
  • User IDs (for moderation logs and Plus status)
  • Channel IDs (for ticket and verification systems)

Data Usage

We do not sell your data. Data is used only to provide moderation services, music playback, and technical support.

Data Retention

Moderation logs are kept as long as the bot is in your server. You can request data deletion by contacting us via our Support Server.
